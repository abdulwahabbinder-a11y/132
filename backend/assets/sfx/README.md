# Transition SFX

Drop your transition sound effects here:

- `whoosh.mp3` — short ~0.8s air whoosh, used between scene cuts.
- `deep_boom.mp3` — ~1.2s sub-bass impact for chapter breaks.

Recommended sources: **Soundsnap**, **Epidemic Sound**, or
**freesound.org** (filter to CC0). Files referenced by
`backend/app/workers/video_pipeline.py`.
